Q.1 DEFINE ECOSYSTEM GIVE EXAMPLE OF NATURAL AND ARTIFICIAL ECOSYSTEM

ANS - ECOSYSTEM:AN ECOSYSTEM IS A STRUCTRAL AND FUNCTIONAL UNIT OF NATURE.it comprises all abiotic and biotic factor
abiotic factor consist of all inorganic material -air ,water and soil
biotic factor consist producer ,consumer,and decomposer

based on source of nutrition every organism takes place in an ecosystem as producer ,consumer and decomposer.

Ecosystems are classified as artificial or natural primarily based on the level of human interference and creation.

NATURAL ECOSYSTEM

Ecosystems are classified as artificial or natural primarily based on the level of human interference and creation. Natural ecosystems develop on their own without human intervention, are self-sustaining, and characterized by high biodiversity for example-river,sea ,forest etc.

ARTIFICIAL ECOSYSTEM

artificial ecosystems are man-made, require constant human maintenance, and often have limited biodiversity. for example-aquarium,agriculture field,zoo ,etc
